Surface Water Assessments

Surface Water Assessments do this and that so here you go.

Overview

Effective water planning and management requires an accurate assessment of the location and quantity of the water resources of the State. SCDNR and SCDHEC are developing surface-water quantity models that simulate the surface water system for each of the eight major watersheds, or basins, in South Carolina. In July 2014, CDM Smith, Inc. was awarded a contract to develop the models for the state.

Surface Water Models

The South Carolina Department of Natural Resources (SCDNR) is pleased to announce the public release of the Simplified Water Allocation Models (SWAM) for the Edisto, Saluda and Salkehatchie river basins. Find out more and download the SWAM models here.

Streamflow and Lake Levels

Find water level data for streams and lakes in South Carolina.

Streamflow Data

The U.S. Geological Survey operates and maintains a network of real-time stream gages across the State. The SCDNR currently supports funding for 16 of these gages. Data from these sites are used to support drought assessments, hydrologic modeling and water planning activities in the State.
